Why UI/UX Matters

💡 Real-World Good UI/UX

  • Apple: Clean, consistent, obvious design patterns.
  • Duolingo: Gamified experience makes it addictive and intuitive.
  • Google Search: One box. One goal. Zero clutter.

😖 Bad UI/UX in the Wild

  • 🛑 Government forms with 25+ fields per page.
  • 🌀 Websites with 10+ pop-ups before you can read anything.
  • 🎯 Captchas that ask you to tap *EVERY* crosswalk… 3 times.

📊 Quick Stats That’ll Blow Your Mind

  • 88% of users won’t return after a bad experience.
  • 70% of shopping carts are abandoned because of bad UX.
  • A better button design earned Amazon $300 million in one year!

🧭 “Good” Depends on Context

Good design isn’t one-size-fits-all.

  • Instagram should feel fun and visual.
  • A hospital app should feel secure and serious.
  • A banking site should be boring (but safe and predictable!).

🎯 3 Tips for Better UI/UX

  1. Keep it simple. Avoid clutter and confusion.
  2. Make things obvious. Labels and buttons should be clear.
  3. Test it. Ask your friends to try it and watch what confuses them.
